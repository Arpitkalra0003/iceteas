Omvana

Hundreds of customizable tracks for your peace of mind in any situation – all in one beautiful app.

Omvana is a personal growth and meditation mobile app (it hit the #1 spot in Health & Fitness in over 30 countries) and web player designed to help you sleep, relax, meditate, get inspired or increase your productivity, giving you instant access to an extensive collection of transformative tracks and audios. You can even customize your sessions by mixing an ambient or background sound with a narrated track using the in-app Omvana mixer.

TribeLearn

Connecting the likeminded in a peer-to-peer learning environment to share best practices and grow together.

Many of the top jobs in the world today barely existed 10 years ago. And education has not caught up. Tribe Learn is a solution to allow people in unique, new fields to create powerful learning opportunities. Think areas such as professional blogging, life coaching, PPC advertising and digital content.

TribeLearn is about connecting people in the same career together through a peer to peer learning platform, so you can learn from people doing the same thing as you all around the world – the best practices, correct attitudes, productivity hacks, and new innovations in your field.
